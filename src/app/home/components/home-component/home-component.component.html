The Book-A-Box concept was conceived to fit most of your small precious items or documents and holds upto a maximum weight of 20 Kg. Each box can be locked on both sides with your own locks to ensure you are the only authorized person to see or access the contents within. We also allow you to label the boxes, paried with a unique box ID to ensure we bring you the right box whenever you need it. Thereafter your box is always just a call away and delivered to your premises at your convenience.
